What is Human Factors?

When I mention my field, people rarely know what I mean. Human Factors? Is that like human resources? Is it I/O psychology? The goal of this page is to help explain what we actually do in this fascinating, relevant, poorly-named discipline.

Here are a few pithy ways I've heard the field described:

  • Human Factors uses what we know about cognitive psychology (memory, attention, etc.) to design better systems. (Tyler Shaw)
  • Unlike some aspects of psychology where if you get it wrong, people might feel bad, if we (Human Factors engineers) get it wrong, people get dead. (Deak Helton)
  • Human Factors aims to optimize the relationship between humans and technology.
